**WE THE PEOPLE** of the Federal Republic of Nigeria,  
**HAVING FIRMLY AND SOLEMNLY RESOLVED:**

> To live in unity and harmony as one indivisible and indissoluble  
> Sovereign Nation under God, dedicated to:
> - The promotion of inter-African solidarity
> - The advancement of world peace
> - International cooperation and understanding

**AND TO PROVIDE** for a Constitution that shall:
> - Promote good government and welfare of all persons  
>   on principles of **Freedom**, **Equality**, and **Justice**
> - Consolidate the unity of our people

**DO HEREBY MAKE, ENACT, AND GIVE TO OURSELVES**  
**THIS CONSTITUTION:**
